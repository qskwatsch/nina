Just a few steps from the walls of Treviso's historical center you will find Nina 7, a comfortable villa from early '900 surrounded by the green of a quiet residential area.

Nina 7 has 3 rooms with private bathroom, a breakfast room, a relax area and a flourishing garden finely furnished

Our breakfast includes the traditional cornetto and cappuccino and a variety of handmade cakes and biscuits, freshly baked bread, freshly made fruit and veggie juices all prepared with organic ingredients coming from the nearby areas.

Ilaria will wait for you travellers with your stories and make you feel like at home.
