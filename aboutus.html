This house has always belonged to our family and aunt Nina had been living here for almost 100 years. The magnolia that grows high and strong in front of the house, was planted when she was a child and still keeps her memory alive.

Aunt Nina, here at number 7, used to receive friends and foreigners for the pleasure of welcoming and sharing.

TodayIlaria, Nina's great-grandniece, welcomes you in this renovated space so that your passage may keep the ancient rooms animated with new stories, as Nina tought us to do.
