By car Motorway A27 Mestre-Belluno, connected to A4 Torino-Trieste, take exit Treviso Sud. Drive direction Castelfranco/Vicenza, at the roundabout, take the first exit on the right, direction Centro. Drive all the external town detour and turn right into Viale Luzzatti. After 100 meters, turn right into via Carrer and right again into Via d'Azeglio. At the end of via d'Azeglio, turn right into Via Rosmini. Free private car park.

By Train Stop at Treviso Centrale Station. Free transfer upon request.

By Plane You can land at Treviso Canova airport or Venice Marco Polo. Free transfer upon request.
